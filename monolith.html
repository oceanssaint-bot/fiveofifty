07 — RULES
07 — Variant Reference
OTHER VARIANTS

Two additional logo variants developed alongside the approved primary — each serving a distinct context.

Variant B — Circle Lock-up

Circle lock-up with dashed gap — for digital avatars, app icons, and profile images. Holds presence at small sizes.

Variant C — Mark Only

Stripped monogram without the text ring — for favicon, stamp, and icon-only contexts where the seal cannot render at sufficient size.

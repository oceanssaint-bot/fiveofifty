Maintain minimum clear space equal to the cap-height of the wordmark (X) on all sides. Never clip the icon or truncate the wordmark.

07 — Motion Identity
LOGO ANIMATION

The animated ident is the canonical motion expression of the brand — intro and outro on all produced video content.

Duration
3–5 sec

Recommended for pre-roll and end-slate on all branded video content.

Placement
Intro / End

Opens and closes all produced work. Never overlay over key content mid-edit.
